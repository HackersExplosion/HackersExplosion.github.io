What is Hacker’s Explosion?


$ Hacker’s explosion is a unique software development conference for people inspired by their desire to solve the problem, and people who want to make pragmatic choices for concrete problems, general problems, common stacks of tasks or unusual challenging situations.

$ Today's open source nature of software development brings us to the variety of implementation options for all kinds of tasks. The idea of the conference is to create the environment for critical thinking in architecture and pragmatic process in making technical decisions.

$ The price of the mistake is way too big to rely on random or insufficiently investigated technologies - we can only start feeling determined after thoroughly comparing and analyzing the alternative solutions.

$ The same technology might be good in one use case and bad in another. Some will suit for small team versus the big one. Another technology can be ideal for certain size budget.

$ To choose the right one we shoud know what axis of "right" we are looking for - exclusively professional design and ease of support? Budget or expences? Speed of development? Cost of maintenance? Flexibility and extensibility?

Conference Philosophy

$ Truth is born of arguments.
$ Thought thrives on conflict.
$ Right decisions are sprout in discussion.

$ Conference format is aiming to faciliate sharing experiences of success and failure, showing technical aspects of some technologies, which your project will benefit from and uncovering the shady parts of other technologies that will lead your solution into complete and bottomless nowhere in some use cases.

$ Speakers will talk arguments. Talk about things they love and prove why it works. Talk about things they hate and have arguments to defend their position.

$ But... What if someone disagree with what is being talkked about on the stage? Of course, speakers might forget to justify their use of some technology or approach with solid arguments. Or they might be just wrong. If so, they will be told so and they will be given a chance to change their opinion or prove their point again.

$ Hacker's Explosion is an interactive conference where attendees and other speakers will have a simple way to show their opinion and agreement with certain ideas broadcasted from the stage in real time. The percentage of audience support will be publicly visible, and if it is low - speaker may pause the talk to answer the questions from the audience.

$ Any technical disagreements when one party has one opinion and another party has the oppisite opinion can be solved having argument based discussion. After that either one of the parties might change the opinion based on the arguments of ther one and parties agree, or the disagreement might be counted as preference-based, which means, it doesn't make sense.
