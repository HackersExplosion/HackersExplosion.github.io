What is Hacker’s Explosion?


hacker’s explosion is a unique software development conference for people inspired by their desire to solve the problem, and people who want to make pragmatic choices for concrete problems, general problems, common stacks of tasks or unusual challenging situations.

today's open source nature of software development brings us to the variety of implementation options for all kinds of tasks. The idea of the conference is to create the environment for critical thinking in architecture and pragmatic process in making technical decisions. The price of the mistake today is way too big to rely on random or insufficiently investigated technologies - we can only start feeling determined after thoroughly comparing and analyzing the alternative solutions.
